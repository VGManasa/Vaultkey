How it works

2FA

Your master password is used only on your device to derive the encryption key. We never see it, never store it, and can never recover it.

Every password you save is encrypted before it leaves your browser. What reaches our servers is an opaque encrypted blob — meaningless without your key.

Even if our entire database were exposed, an attacker would have no plaintext passwords — only ciphertext protected by 390,000 rounds of key-stretching.

Only your master password can decrypt your vault. Not us. Not anyone else.
